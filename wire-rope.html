Manufacturer of Custom Wire Rope Assemblies

Flash Cutting - Hydralic Swaging - Cable Stripping - Heat Stamping - Pull Testing - Kitting

Aero Assemblies has over 35 years' experience manufacturing custom wire rope cable assemblies for the aerospace, industrial and commercial industries. From simple to intricate applications, we parnter with our customers to fabricate their assemblies to their required specificatoins using cost effective solutions. We offer an extensive inventory of wire rope along with standard and specialized fittings ranging from 3/64" to 3/8". Our strategic purchasing and inventory management gives us the ability to provide competitive pricing along with reliable production times to our customers.

We can provide First Article of Inspection, Pull Test Certification, Certificate of Compliance, Material Certification, lot traceablity, specialized labeling and other services upon request.
